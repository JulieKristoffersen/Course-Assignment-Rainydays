About us

Rainyday is a brand established in 2014 by the couple Smith. An active couple that wanted to make it possible to stay outside no matter the weather or activities. The company has now reached out to over 100,000 active people wanting to achieve their next goal in perfect clothing.

We are located in Washington, DC, and hopefully, we will open more stores in the future.
